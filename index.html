Built-in Strategies

Three strategies. One harness.

Start with paper trading. Graduate to live. Every strategy runs the same monitor-evaluate-execute loop.

Trailing Stop

Set a floor that only moves up. As your stock climbs, the floor climbs with it. If it drops below your trailing threshold, the bot sells automatically. Locks in gains, caps losses.

Beginner

Ladder Buys

When a stock dips, buy more at each level to average down your cost basis. Set the rungs, set the budget, and the bot executes each buy automatically as thresholds hit.

Intermediate

The Wheel

Sell cash-secured puts to collect premium. If assigned, sell covered calls on the shares. Collect income regardless of direction. The bot tracks strikes, rolls contracts, manages the cycle.

Advanced

The Cooperative Layer

Protection that belongs to everyone.

Trading bots extract from each other. FloorPricer members build shared intelligence that compounds for the collective.

Shared Strategy Library

Members contribute strategies. The collective backtests and improves them. Like open source, but for trading logic. Every member benefits from every other member's insight.

Collective Risk Monitor

If 40% of members are concentrated in the same sector, the system flags systemic risk to the community. Your trailing stop protects you. The collective radar protects everyone.

Hard Intercept Guardrails

The AI cannot execute above your stated risk tolerance without explicit human confirmation. Same physician-grade attestation pattern used in healthcare. Your rules are inviolable.

Cooperative Dividends

Platform revenue goes back to members, not shareholders. As the collective grows, the intelligence compounds and the cost per member drops. Your success funds everyone's tools.
